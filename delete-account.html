Delete Your Account

Follow the steps below to permanently delete your Festival Foodie account and all data associated with it.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Festival Foodie
    Launch the Festival Foodie app on your iOS or Android device and make sure you are signed in to the account you wish to delete.
  2. Open the Menu
    Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) icon in the top-right corner of the home screen.
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
    Select the "Delete Account" option (shown in red) from the dropdown menu.
  4. Confirm Deletion
    A confirmation dialog will appear asking if you are sure. Tap "Delete" to confirm. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

⚠ Before You Delete

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.
  • You will be signed out immediately and will no longer be able to log in with the same credentials.
  • Any content you have posted (posts, comments, chat messages, reviews) will be removed.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, profile images)
  • Your organization details (organization name and address)
  • Your food stall listings and menu items
  • Your dish reviews, ratings, and attached photos
  • Your social posts, comments, and likes
  • Your chat messages and chat room memberships
  • Your group chat participation and admin roles
  • Your push notification token and preferences
  • Your Firebase authentication credentials
  • Your locally stored session data (authentication token, cached preferences)
